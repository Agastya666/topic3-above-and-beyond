Nissan Skyline R43

Car Overview

The Nissan Skyline R34 is a sports car that was produced by the Japanese automaker Nissan from 1999 to 2002. It is part of the Skyline GT-R series, which has a reputation for high performance and advanced technology.

Car Features

  • Offered in several trim levels, including the base model, GT, GT-X, and high-performance GT-R
  • Powered by a 2.6-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six engine
  • Produced up to 276 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque
  • Featured Nissan's advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel drive system
  • Had adjustable rear wing and NACA ducts on the hood for improved aerodynamics
  • Equipped with Super HICAS four-wheel steering system and Active LSD for optimal handling
  • Distinctive look with rounded front end and sharp, angular lines
  • Well-appointed interior with driver-focused cockpit and sporty seats
