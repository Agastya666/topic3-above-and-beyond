Car's discription:
Pagani Zonda R

Car Overview

The Pagani Zonda R is a limited edition, high-performance sports car manufactured by Pagani Automobili, an Italian supercar manufacturer. The Zonda R is the track-focused variant of the Pagani Zonda, designed to deliver the ultimate driving experience for high-speed track use.

Car Features

  • Mid-mounted 6.0-liter V12 engine, developed by Mercedes-AMG
  • Produces 750 horsepower and 710 lb-ft of torque
  • 6-speed sequential gearbox
  • Exterior design focused on aerodynamics and performance
  • Carbon-titanium chassis for strength and lightness
  • Stripped-down cabin with carbon-fiber dashboard, racing seats, and roll cage
  • Data acquisition system to record and display telemetry data from the car's sensors
  • Only 15 units produced, making it highly exclusive and sought-after
