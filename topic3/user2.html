Toyota Supra MK4

Car Overview

The Toyota Supra MK4 is a high-performance sports car that was produced by Toyota from 1993 to 2002. It is widely regarded as a classic among car enthusiasts, thanks to its sleek styling, impressive performance, and legendary status in the import car scene.

Car Features

  • Powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine (2JZ-GTE)
  • Engine produces 320 horsepower and 315 lb-ft of torque
  • 6-speed manual transmission (4-speed automatic also available)
  • Rear-wheel drive with limited-slip differential
  • 0-60 mph in around 4.6 seconds
  • Top speed of 155 mph (electronically limited)
  • Double wishbone suspension at front and rear
  • Driver-focused cockpit with power-adjustable leather seats
  • Climate control and premium audio system
  • Dual airbags and anti-lock brakes for safety
  • Sleek exterior design with long hood and short rear deck
  • Rounded body lines, integrated headlights, and distinctive spoiler
